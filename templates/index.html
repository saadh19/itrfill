Frequently Asked Questions

Is my IT portal password safe?

Yes. Your password is encrypted with AES-256 encryption the moment you enter it. It is used only once to log in and file your ITR, then deleted immediately. It is never stored in any database.

Why do I still need to enter an OTP?

The Income Tax Department requires Aadhaar OTP verification as the final step. This is a government security requirement. Only the OTP can be sent to your phone — no bot can receive it. Everything else is automated.

What if I don't want to give my IT portal password?

No problem. Leave the password field empty. We will generate your ITR-1 XML file and you can upload it manually on incometax.gov.in — takes about 3 minutes.

Who is this for?

Salaried employees with a single employer filing ITR-1. If you have business income, capital gains, or multiple employers, you need ITR-2 or ITR-3 — consult a CA.

When does my refund arrive?

Typically 15–45 days after filing. The IT department processes refunds and credits directly to your bank account linked with PAN.
