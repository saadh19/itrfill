Refund Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025  ·  We believe in fair and transparent refunds.

Summary

ITRFill charges ₹299 per ITR-1 filing. Since we provide a digital service that involves AI processing and immediate delivery of your ITR XML file, our refund policy is specific about when refunds are applicable.

If there is a technical failure on our end that prevents you from getting your ITR XML, you are entitled to a full refund. No questions asked.

When You Are Eligible for a Refund

✅ Eligible for refund

  • Payment deducted but XML not generated
  • AI failed to read your Form 16
  • Technical error on our server
  • Duplicate payment charged
  • Service was unavailable at time of payment
  • Wrong amount charged

❌ Not eligible for refund

  • XML generated and downloaded successfully
  • Changed your mind after receiving XML
  • Uploaded wrong Form 16 yourself
  • ITR rejected by IT dept due to your error
  • Requesting refund after 7 days
  • Dissatisfied with tax amount calculated

Special Cases

Payment deducted but XML not received

This is the most common issue. If your payment was successful but you did not receive the XML download, contact us immediately with your Razorpay payment ID. We will either generate your XML manually or process a full refund within 24 hours.

Form 16 not read correctly by AI

If our AI extracted incorrect data from your Form 16 and you noticed before downloading, contact us. We will manually review and correct your filing at no extra charge. If correction is not possible, a full refund will be issued.

Duplicate payment

If you were charged twice for the same filing, we will refund the duplicate amount within 3 working days after verification.

When in doubt, contact us. We would rather give you a refund than have an unhappy customer.

Refund Timeline

  • Refund request received: We acknowledge within 24 hours
  • Verification: 1-2 working days
  • Refund initiated: Within 3 working days of approval
  • Credit to your account: 5-7 working days (depends on your bank)
  • UPI refunds: Usually within 24-48 hours
  • Card refunds: 5-7 working days
  • Net banking: 3-5 working days

Refunds are processed to the original payment method only. We cannot refund to a different account.

Important Note About Income Tax Refunds

The refund mentioned in this policy refers to our ₹299 service fee only.

The income tax refund you see in your ITR (e.g. ₹19,000 refund from IT department) is a separate matter handled entirely by the Income Tax Department of India. ITRFill has no control over when the IT department processes and credits your tax refund to your bank account.

ITRFill cannot be held responsible for delays in income tax refunds from the IT department. For IT refund status, check incometax.gov.in.
