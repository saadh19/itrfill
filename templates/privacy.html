ITRFill ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our ITR filing service at itrfill.onrender.com.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Form 16 PDF (uploaded for processing)
  • PAN number
  • Name, employer name
  • Bank account number and IFSC code
  • Income Tax portal password (if provided for auto-filing)
  • Deduction details (rent, home loan, investments)

Payment information

We use Razorpay for payment processing. We do NOT store your card details, UPI ID, or net banking credentials. All payment data is handled directly by Razorpay and governed by their privacy policy.

Technical information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • Reading your Form 16 to extract tax data using AI
  • Calculating your income tax liability under old and new regime
  • Generating your ITR-1 XML file
  • Auto-filing on incometax.gov.in (if you provide IT portal password)
  • Processing your payment
  • Sending you your ITR XML file
  • Responding to your support queries

We never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than filing your ITR.

3. Data Storage and Security

Form 16 PDF

Your Form 16 PDF is processed in memory and automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour of upload. We do not store your PDF permanently.

IT Portal Password

If you provide your Income Tax portal password for auto-filing, it is encrypted using AES-256 encryption immediately upon entry. It is used once to log in and file your ITR, then permanently deleted. It is never stored in any database.

Generated XML file

Your ITR-1 XML file is stored temporarily for download and deleted within 24 hours.

Security measures

  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • HTTPS/SSL for all data transmission
  • No permanent storage of financial documents
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

Service providers

  • Groq AI — for processing Form 16 text (no personally identifiable data stored by Groq)
  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Render/Railway — cloud hosting provider

Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required by Indian law, court order, or government authority.

We never share your PAN, salary details, or tax information with any third party for commercial purposes.

  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Grievance — raise a complaint with our grievance officer

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

6. Cookies

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings but some features may not work correctly.

7. Children's Privacy

ITRFill is intended for adults who are income tax filers. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has used our service, please contact us immediately and we will delete their data.
