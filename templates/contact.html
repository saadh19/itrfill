Common Questions

My payment was deducted but I did not get the XML file

Please email us with your PAN number and payment ID from Razorpay. We will manually process your filing within 2 hours and send the XML to your email.

The AI did not read my Form 16 correctly

Some scanned or image-based Form 16 PDFs may not parse correctly. Please contact us on WhatsApp with your Form 16 and we will manually process it for you at no extra charge.

I need a refund

Please read our Refund Policy page. If you are eligible, email us with your order details and we will process the refund within 5-7 working days.

Can I file ITR for my family members?

Yes. Each filing is a separate transaction. You can file for as many people as you want — each costs ₹299.
